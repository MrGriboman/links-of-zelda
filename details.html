Link

The Hero of Hyrule · Bearer of the Triforce of Courage

Link is the eternal champion of the Triforce of Courage, destined across countless ages to stand against the darkness threatening Hyrule. A young Hylian of few words but indomitable spirit, he wields the Master Sword — the Blade of Evil's Bane — and carries the hopes of an entire kingdom upon his shoulders. Whether born as a forest child in Kokiri Woods or awakened from a century-long slumber, Link's legend endures.

Zelda

Princess of Hyrule · Bearer of the Triforce of Wisdom

Princess Zelda is the wise and graceful heir to the Hyrulean throne, bearer of the sacred Triforce of Wisdom. A gifted scholar and practitioner of ancient sealing magic, she carries the bloodline of the goddess Hylia herself. Though often portrayed as a figure to be rescued, Zelda is a formidable force in her own right — a strategist, prophetess, and guardian who shapes the fate of the Sacred Realm across every age.

Ganondorf

The Demon King · Bearer of the Triforce of Power

Ganondorf is the King of Evil, the Gerudo sorcerer who obtained the Triforce of Power and bent it to his will. Born once each century to the all-female Gerudo tribe, he turned his gift of leadership into an instrument of conquest. Driven by a relentless hunger for dominion over Hyrule, he has clashed with the Hero and the Princess across every cycle of the eternal triangle. In his most monstrous form, he becomes Ganon — the Dark Beast.

Impa

Guardian of the Royal Family · Shadow Warrior

Impa is the eternal guardian of the Hyrulean royal family, a shadow warrior of the ancient Sheikah clan. Fiercely loyal and formidably skilled, she has protected Princess Zelda across countless generations — sometimes as a stoic elder, sometimes as a battle-hardened warrior. The Sheikah are Hyrule's eye in the dark, and Impa is their most trusted blade. Her red eyes hold centuries of devotion and duty.

Midna

Twilight Princess · Ruler of the Twilight Realm

Midna is the impish, quick-tongued ruler of the Twilight Realm, cursed into an imp form by the usurper Zant. Cynical and self-serving at first, she formed an unlikely bond with Link over their shared journey through shadow and light. Behind her sardonic wit lies a fierce and heartbroken queen — one who ultimately chose sacrifice over dominion. She is perhaps the most complex companion in the entire Zelda series.

Navi

Fairy Companion · Voice of the Great Deku Tree

Navi is the fairy companion sent by the Great Deku Tree to guide the young Link on his first great adventure. Radiant with purpose and tireless in encouragement, she introduced a generation of players to Hyrule with her luminous chime and urgent cries. Though often the subject of fond parody, Navi's guidance was genuine — she gave Link his eyes in the darkness, and her absence at the game's end resonated with quiet sorrow.
